Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

FindGo is built with a privacy-first approach. This policy explains how we handle information within the FindGo mobile application.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • Location Data: FindGo requires access to your device’s Precise Location to pin your car’s position and show your current location relative to your vehicle.
  • No Server Storage: We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any external servers. All coordinates and parking pins are stored locally on your device.
  • No Accounts: You are not required to create an account or provide any personal information (name, email, or phone number) to use FindGo.

2. Third-Party Services

Navigation: When you request directions, FindGo passes your saved coordinates to your choice of third-party maps (e.g., Google Maps or Apple Maps). These services operate under their own independent privacy policies.

3. Notifications

We use local notifications for the parking timer alerts. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and are not used for tracking or marketing purposes.
